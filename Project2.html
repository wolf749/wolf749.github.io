Procedural Curves 6/5/16


This project is my implementation of Dongsung Huh's paper on The Vector Space of Convex Curves: How to Mix Shapes

This paper outlines a really cool idea: how to add curves together in a visually native way. The math here is right out of differentiable geometry, where the log-radius is specified by a period function. I came across this paper while trying to devise a way to pick a procedurally generate a castle. In particular, how to generate the outline a castle should occupy [walls, towers, and the like can follow pretty naturally once the outline is specified].
