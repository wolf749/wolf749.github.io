Infection Game

You can get a better feel for this project by clicking to the left and trying to enclose the red squares with the blue.

I wrote this partially as a way to get familiar with javascript, and partially because of the myriad of zombie outbreak movies showing in 2014/2015. Image an outbreak occurs and there's limited supplies to enclose it, how should you distribute your supplies? For this particular setup the best possible solution is to enclose 12 red tiles, try to beat it!
